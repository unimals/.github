# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Unimals is committed to a respectful, practical, and constructive environment.

We expect participants to:

- communicate with clarity and respect
- debate ideas without personal attacks
- assume good intent while giving direct feedback
- focus on building useful things together

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- personal attacks or abusive language
- doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- disruptive behavior that repeatedly derails collaboration

## Enforcement

Organization maintainers may remove, edit, or moderate contributions and participation that violate these standards.
